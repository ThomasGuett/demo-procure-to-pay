Procurement Guidelines

Our procurement process ensures all purchases align with company policies and budget allocations. All requests must be approved by department heads and finance before processing.

  • Requests under $500 will be automatically approved
  • Requests between $500-$5,000 require department head approval
  • Requests above $5,000 require additional CFO approval
  • Maximum approval time: 3 business days
Please note: All requests must include a clear business justification and expected ROI. Incomplete forms will be rejected and returned for revision.
